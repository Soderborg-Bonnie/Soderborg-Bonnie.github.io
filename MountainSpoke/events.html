Cycle the Continental Divide

Cycle the Continental Divide is an epic journey through some of the prettiest country in the world. We start in Bozeman and head northwest. We'll cross the Continental Divide the first time as we're heading east to west. We'll loop back to Bozeman and cross the Divide again heading west to east. In between, we'll cover around 350 miles. 2018 marks the eighteenth year of our annual trip and will be held August 13-19. That's seven days of riding and playing in the great outdoor playground of Montana. Montana's not called "Big Sky" for nothing! We recommend you are at least an intermediate rider on the higher end of the scale . We should probably say you should be an advanced rider, but if you're an intermediate rider with a lot of grit, we think you can handle it. This is riding the back roads of Montana, and as such, you'll want a mountain bike and a helmet in tip top condition. We'll want $1250 to make this trip happen for you. That is all-inclusive. Some pretty tasty meals all-inclusive. Here's our agenda for this year:
