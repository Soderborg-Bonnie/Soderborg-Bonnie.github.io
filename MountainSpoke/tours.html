Adventure Tours

Museum of the Rockies Tour

Join us for a family-friendly ride to the Museum of the Rockies.The MOR is world famous for its dinosaur fossils, including a full scale T-Rex,and worthy of its own ride! This ride is completely in town and is rated for beginners. We'll ride from the shop to the museum with a few stops along the way to check out a couple of historical places around Bozeman. Cost is $42/adults and $30/kids. The length of the ride is 8.8 miles and begins at 9am. We'll arrive at the museum around 9:40am, check out the dinosaur bones and more and then eat lunch at 12:30pm. You can choose from several delicious sandwiches or salads from the Pickle Barrel who'll be packing box lunches for us. After lunch we'll head back to the shop and should be there around 1:30pm.This ride a tradition for many local families, as well as a favorite of many tourists. Come join us!

Join us for some fresh mountain air as we ride the Mountain to Meadow trail from the base of Big Sky Resort. We'll meet in the northeast corner of the parking lot of the resort at 10am. The trail is 5.8 miles with a slight downhill most of the way. The views are spectacular! The mountain trees and meadow flowers fill the air with their fragant aroma. Your sense will be delighted! The is a beginner level trail. Cost is $20/rider. This is a beauty you don't want to miss.

If You Dare Tour

This tour is for the hardcore, cycling enthusiast not afraid of heights. We'll be riding the Bangtail Divide which is an advanced level ride of about 24 miles. We'll meet at the shop at 8am, load the bikes on the trialer and hop in the shuttle van. Cost is $50/rider and includes a box lunch from the Pickle Barrel. We'll return to the shop around 2pm. Join us if you dare!
